Posture Paywall does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data is processed locally on your device.

1. Overview

Posture Paywall ("Extension", "Service") prioritizes your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the extension handles your information.

2. Information We Collect

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal Identifiable Information (Name, Email, Phone, etc.)
  • Location Data
  • Browsing History
  • Webcam Video or Images
  • Login Credentials or Account Data

Information Processed Locally

The following information is processed solely on your device and is never transmitted externally:

  • Webcam Data: Analyzed in real-time for posture detection and immediately discarded. Never saved.
  • Posture Statistics: Daily/Weekly posture history stored in Chrome Local Storage.
  • Settings: User preferences (sensitivity, blur intensity, etc.) stored locally.

3. How We Process Data

Posture Paywall uses computer vision technology to analyze posture completely offline within your browser.

  • All processing is performed directly on your device.
  • Webcam feed is analyzed in real-time and immediately discarded.
  • Only the analysis result (posture score) is stored in local storage.
  • Works perfectly without an internet connection.

4. Data Storage

All data is stored on your device using Chrome's chrome.storage.local API:

  • Posture Statistics (Last 7 days)
  • User Settings
  • Session History (Last 10 sessions)

You can delete all stored data at any time by uninstalling the extension.

5. Third-Party Sharing

Posture Paywall does not share any data with third parties. There is absolutely no data collection for analysis, advertising, or marketing purposes.

6. Permissions

The extension requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • Camera: Required for posture detection.
  • Storage: To save settings and statistics.
  • Offscreen: For background posture analysis.
  • Notifications: For break reminders (optional).

7. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated as needed. Significant changes will be notified within the extension.
